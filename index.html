COVID-19 FAQ

From which country did COVID-19 originate?

COVID-19 was first identified in the city of Wuhan, Hubei province, China, in December 2019.

What does 'COVID-19' stand for?

'COVID-19' stands for 'Coronavirus Disease 2019,' referring to the year when the disease was first identified.

What are the common symptoms of COVID-19?

Common symptoms include fever, cough, fatigue, loss of taste or smell, and difficulty breathing among others.

How can COVID-19 be prevented?

Preventive measures include wearing masks, practicing hand hygiene, maintaining social distance, and getting vaccinated.

Can COVID-19 be transmitted from people who are asymptomatic?

Yes, individuals who do not show symptoms (asymptomatic) can still transmit the virus to others.
